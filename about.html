About Eyedontbyte

Your destination for engaging STEM education content.

Our Mission

Welcome to Eyedontbyte, where we take the "boring" out of STEM! Here, we’re on a mission to make science, technology, engineering, and math as accessible and fun as your favorite video game. Our goal? To make sure everyone—from absolute beginners to seasoned experts—has a blast while leveling up their STEM skills. No puzzles to solve, just pure learning magic (okay, maybe a few puzzles, but we promise they're fun).

Who We Are

Eyedontbyte is a one-person operation, run by someone who *really* loves science, tech, and making learning feel like a joyride. Think of me as your friendly neighborhood guide, here to help you navigate the labyrinth of STEM with zero stress, a lot of curiosity, and maybe even some bad jokes along the way. Whether you're tinkering with code, dabbling in science experiments, or obsessing over the latest tech, I'm your personal guide to all things STEM. Just don’t ask me to solve the world’s energy crisis... yet.

Our Content

Expect a variety of content that’s as diverse as a tech conference after-party: programming tutorials, mind-blowing science experiments, tech reviews, and deep dives into futuristic topics. Think of it as the "choose-your-own-adventure" of learning, where curiosity is your compass, and fun is the destination. We don’t just teach—we create a community where questions are celebrated, answers are shared, and every lesson feels like a discovery. Get ready to unlock your potential—no cake required!
